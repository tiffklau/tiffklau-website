SmartQ

SmartQ is an app created as a school project for PriceSmart Foods. PriceSmart Foods is a regional chain of supermarkets located in BC, Canada. The purpose of this project was to identify an existing problem and to design an app to resolve it.

Challenges

Seeing long line-ups at the check out can be frustrating for customers, even discouraging purchases and recurring visits. With this problem in mind, I created a number ticket machine mobile app that allows customers to wait for their turn to check out while still shopping.

My Role
  • User Experience Design
  • User Interface Design
  • Visual Design
  • Wireframe
  • Mock Ups
Branding

The original color theme, green, blue and white were kept for easy association to the PriceSmart Foods brand. The logo was designed with a fun play on the letter Q with the word ‘queue’.

Process

Understanding customers’ shopping process is essential. After surveying and interviewing target users, creating personas and journey maps, I discovered rich insights that inspired me on creating this SmartQ App. The process involved a lot of iterations and user flow testings to ensure users get a seamless navigation. Finally, I took PriceSmart Foods brand and created high fidelity mockups.

Persona and User Journey

The objective of a persona and user journey was to confirm our target audience and to identify their needs.

Sitemap and Userflow

The goal was to have a simple and clean navigation. Users range from teenagers to seniors from different nationalities and backgrounds. Therefore, I designed the app to recycle pages to keep consistency throughout.
