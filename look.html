Look

Look is created for Jactrend, an online store that offers local fashion brands from Asia and North America, to encourage users to try different looks and brands. It is a lifestyle app designed for men to get outfit inspirations when they cannot decide on what to wear for that day or for a specific event. With this app, you will receive an outfit inspiration everyday based on the weather at your location. You may also customize the look by choosing specific events or specific items that you want to wear.

Challenges

To design an app that consists of numerous options to choose yet must be easy to navigate with minimal steps and fun to use.
