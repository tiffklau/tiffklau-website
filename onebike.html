OneBike

OneBike is a developmental learning project. It is a fictional bike shop that sells innovative bicycles. This product website was created to sell Denny Bike, a real-life Bike Design Project-winning bike.

This is a school project made for the Interaction Design Essentials program at Emily Carr University of Art + Design. We were assigned to design and code a website of our choice to demonstrate our skills based on what we learned in class. The Denny logo and all images and contents were taken from Sizemore (http://www.sizemorebicycle.com/#/om-2014-denny/) and The Bike Design Project (http://oregonmanifest.com/and-the-winner-isdenny/).

Challenges

This was my first experiment in coding, and pixel perfecting design and animations. Through this assignment, I now have the skills of coding a responsive site.
