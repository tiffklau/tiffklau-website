hello

I grew up in the beautiful Vancouver, British Columbia. I am a Business Bachelor graduate from Simon Fraser University with an Interaction Design Certificate from Emily Carr University. Along the way to finding my dream job in UI/UX design, I spent five years in the finance industry, which led to a short reallocation to Hong Kong for a fast-paced lifestyle. One day, in the midst of crunching numbers, I decided to quit my job, move home, and follow my heart’s passion. Now, I bring together the best of both worlds to my work, finding ways to apply my business acumen into my design process. I approach every project with the business objectives and needs of users in mind and find the perfect balance between the two.


If you managed to stay on the homepage for 15 seconds, you will have gotten to know a little more about me, and the things I enjoy. Among my favourites, I love creating the most. I feel the most accomplished when I make something from nothing, make the complex simple, and solve a business problem with creativity. The process is always fun, creative and challenging.
